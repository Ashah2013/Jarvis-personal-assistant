Web Queries

Search the web for specific query. Currently supported search engines:

  1. Google (keyword: google)
  2. Bing (keyword: bing)
  3. Yahoo (keyword: yahoo)
  4. search — for general searches, irrespective of the search engine.

Syntax:
keyword {query string}

Videos on Youtube

Search youtube for videos

Syntax:
youtube {query string}
OR
vidoes on {query string}

Images

Search images on the web

Syntax:
image {query string}
OR
images {query string}

Live Weather Conditions

Get live weather condition updates about any place.

Syntax:
weather {city name} {state name}

Word meanings

Accurate word meanings from oxford

Syntax:
meaning {query word}

Details on medicine

Get complete details about the medicine
by quering the generic name of the medicine.

Syntax:
medicine {generic name}
Example:
medicine paracetamol
medicine Rebamipide

Symptoms based medicine advice

Get medicine advice based on the symptoms or condition of the patient.

Syntax:
symptoms {symptom name}
Example:
symptoms headache
symptoms cramps
